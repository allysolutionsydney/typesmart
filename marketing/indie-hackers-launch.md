# Indie Hackers Launch Post

## Title
Launched TypeSmart — AI writing assistant with tone control

## Body

Hey Indie Hackers! 👋

Just launched my first real SaaS and looking for feedback.

**What is it?**
TypeSmart is an AI writing assistant that lets you control the tone of your content. Instead of generic "professional" speak, you get:

• Professional (boardroom-ready)
• Friendly (warm & approachable)
• Assertive (confident & direct)
• Apologetic (delicate situations)
• Enthusiastic (product launches)

**4 built-in tools:**
1. LinkedIn post generator
2. Email rewriter
3. Dating message writer
4. Complaint letter assistant

**The Story**
I built this because I kept rewriting the same content in different tones. An investor update needed to sound professional. A client check-in needed to be friendly. A payment reminder needed to be assertive.

Most AI tools give you one generic tone. I wanted something that adapts to the situation.

**Tech Stack**
• Next.js 16 + Tailwind
• OpenAI GPT-4
• Clerk (auth)
• Supabase (database)
• Stripe (payments)
• Vercel (hosting)

**Current Stats**
• Launched: Today
• Users: 0 (literally just deployed)
• MRR: $0
• Time to build: 1 week

**Pricing**
• Free: 5 generations/day
• Pro: $9/month unlimited

**What I need help with:**
• Feedback on the landing page
• Ideas for distribution
• Feature requests

Try it: typesmart.io

What do you think? Brutal honesty appreciated.

## Follow-up Comments

**If someone asks about tech stack:**
Happy to share! Next.js App Router, Clerk for auth (took 30 min to set up), Supabase for the database, Stripe for payments. Deployed on Vercel. Total dev time was about 40 hours over a week.

**If someone asks about OpenAI costs:**
Good question! Using GPT-4o. Average generation costs ~$0.015. So at $9/month, a user needs to generate 600+ times to cost me money. Most free users do 5-10 generations, so unit economics work. Plus I have rate limiting.
