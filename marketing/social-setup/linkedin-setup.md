# LinkedIn Setup Guide for TypeSmart

## Account Options

### Option 1: Company Page (Recommended)
**Pros:** Professional, scalable, can have multiple admins
**Cons:** Lower organic reach than personal profiles

### Option 2: Personal Profile as Founder
**Pros:** Higher reach, personal connection
**Cons:** Tied to you personally

### Our Recommendation: Both
- **Company Page:** TypeSmart (official brand presence)
- **Personal Profile:** You post as founder + share company updates
