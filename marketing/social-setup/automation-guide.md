## Engagement Automation (Use Carefully)

### Auto-Likes (via PhantomBuster or similar)
- Auto-like posts with hashtags: #buildinpublic #indiedev
- Limit: 50 likes/day to avoid ban

### Auto-Follow
- Follow people who engage with your content
- Unfollow if no follow-back after 7 days

### Auto-DMs (NOT RECOMMENDED)
- Don't auto-DM new followers (spammy)
- Instead: Personal welcome within 24 hours

## Content Repurposing Strategy

### 1 Long Post = 5 Pieces of Content

**Example: LinkedIn Launch Post**

1. **Full post** → LinkedIn (Day 1)
2. **First 3 paragraphs** → Twitter thread hook
3. **One key point** → Twitter standalone tweet
4. **Screenshot of stats** → Instagram (if you add it)
5. **Quote from post** → Quote card for Twitter/LinkedIn

### Weekly Workflow

**Sunday: Content Creation (2 hours)**
- Write 7 LinkedIn posts
- Break into 21 Twitter posts
- Create 3-5 graphics in Canva
- Schedule everything in Buffer

**Daily (15 min):**
- Check mentions and replies
- Engage with 5 relevant posts
- Monitor analytics

## Crisis Management

### If Account Gets Flagged/Limited
1. **Stop all automation immediately**
2. **Post manually for 48 hours**
3. **Reduce engagement activity**
4. **Appeal if suspended**

### Best Practices to Avoid Issues
- Don't post more than 5x/day per platform
- Don't follow more than 100 people/day
- Don't use aggressive auto-DMs
- Always reply to comments personally
- Mix scheduled + real-time content
