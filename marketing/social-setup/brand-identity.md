# TypeSmart Brand Identity Guide

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Personality Traits
- **Helpful but not pushy** — Always offering value, never salesy
- **Smart but accessible** — AI-powered but easy to understand
- **Efficient** — Gets to the point, respects time
- **Friendly professional** — Like a colleague you actually like

### Tone Guidelines
| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Transform your rough ideas into polished writing" | "Our revolutionary AI leverages cutting-edge NLP" |
| "5 free generations per day" | "Leverage our freemium model" |
| "What writing tasks eat up your time?" | "What's your pain point?" |
| "Try it free — no credit card" | "Sign up for our trial offer" |
| "We just shipped a new feature" | "We are pleased to announce..." |

### Writing Style
- Short sentences
- Active voice
- Contractions okay (you'll, we're, it's)
- Emojis sparingly (1-2 per post max)
- No jargon unless explaining it

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Indigo (#6366F1)
- **Secondary:** Purple (#8B5CF6)
- **Accent:** Pink (#EC4899)
- **Background:** Dark slate (#0F172A)
- **Text:** White (#FFFFFF)

### Logo Ideas
Option 1: Stylized "TS" monogram with sparkle/pen icon
Option 2: Minimalist pen with AI circuit patterns
Option 3: Wordmark "TypeSmart" with gradient text

### Profile Image Specs
- **Twitter/X:** 400x400px, circular crop
- **LinkedIn:** 300x300px (company logo), 400x400px (personal)
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background

### Banner/Header Images
- **Twitter/X:** 1500x500px
- **LinkedIn:** 1128x191px (company), 1584x396px (personal)
- **Design:** Dark gradient with product screenshot or abstract AI pattern

- Spammy tags (#follow4follow)
