## Option 1: The Build Story (Personal)

I spent the last week building something that solves a problem I face every week.

Writing LinkedIn posts.

Not the content — I know what I want to say. But finding the right tone? Making it sound professional but not robotic? That's the hard part.

So I built TypeSmart.io

It's an AI writing assistant that takes your rough ideas and transforms them into polished posts, emails, dating messages, even complaint letters — in 5 different tones.

✅ Friendly  
✅ Assertive
✅ Apologetic
✅ Enthusiastic

I use the Professional tone for LinkedIn, Friendly for client emails, and Enthusiastic when I'm announcing something big.
