# Product Hunt Launch - TypeSmart

## Tagline
AI writing assistant with perfect tone control

## Description

TypeSmart transforms your rough ideas into polished writing — in 5 different tones.

**The Problem**
You know what you want to say, but finding the right tone is hard. Too professional sounds robotic. Too casual sounds unprofessional. Generic AI tools give you cookie-cutter content that sounds like everyone else.

**The Solution**
TypeSmart lets you choose your exact tone:

🎯 Professional — Boardroom-ready posts and emails
😊 Friendly — Approachable, warm communication  
💪 Assertive — Confident, direct messaging
🙏 Apologetic — Delicate situations handled with care
🚀 Enthusiastic — Product launches and big announcements

**4 Built-in Tools:**
1. LinkedIn Post Generator — Engaging professional content
2. Email Rewriter — Clear, effective emails in any tone
3. Dating Message Writer — Genuine, engaging first messages
4. Complaint Letter Assistant — Firm but professional complaints

**Pricing:**
• Free: 5 generations per day
• Pro: $9/month unlimited

Built with Next.js, OpenAI GPT-4, Stripe, and Clerk.
